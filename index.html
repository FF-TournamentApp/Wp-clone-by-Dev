Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about WP Clone

Is WP Clone free to use?

Yes, WP Clone is completely free to download and use. There are no hidden charges or subscription fees.

How is WP Clone different from WhatsApp?

WP Clone offers a cleaner interface, faster performance, and additional customization options while maintaining the familiar WhatsApp experience.

Is my data secure with WP Clone?

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption to ensure your messages and media remain private and secure.

Can I use WP Clone on multiple devices?

Currently, WP Clone is designed for Android devices. We're working on iOS and web versions that will allow multi-device usage.

How do I transfer my chats from WhatsApp?

We're developing a chat migration tool. For now, you can manually backup important chats and media before switching to WP Clone.
